Lionel Messi

One of the Greatest Football Players Of All Time

Chronology:

  • 1987: Lionel Andrés Messi Cuccittini was born on 24 June 1987 in Rosario, Santa Fe, Argentina.
  • 1993: At the age of six, he joins Newell's Old Boys.
  • 1997: He is diagnosized with a Growth Hormone Deficiency.
  • 2001: He jois Football Club Barcelona, who takes over his treatment.
  • 2003: At 16 years, four months, and 23 days old, Messi does his first team debut in Barcelona.
  • 2004: Debut with Argentina's national team. First professional contract.
  • 2005: Messi wins the 2005 FIFA World Youth Championship in Netherlands. First contract as a senior team player.
  • 2006: First UEFA Champions League and first appearance in a a World Cup with Argentina.
  • 2008: Messi wins an Olympic gold Medal with Argentina in the Summer Olympics in Beijing.
  • 2009: Second UEFA Champions League and first sextuple with his club in football history. First Ballon d'Or and FIFA World Player of the Year award. Barcelona Wins their first FIFA Club World Cup.
  • 2010: Second appearance in a World Cup. Second Ballon d'Or.
  • 2011: Third UEFA Champions League. Third Ballon d'Or.Second FIFA Club World Cup.
  • 2012: Fourth Ballon d'Or.
  • 2014: Messi loses the World Cup Final against Germany, and is awarded as the best player of the tournament.
  • 2015: Fourth UEFA Champions League. Third FIFA Club World Cup. Fifth Ballon d'Or
  • 2019: Sixth Ballon d'Or. First The Best FIFA Men's Player. Third FIFA Club World Cup.
  • 2021: Messi wins the Copa América with his national team for the first time against Brazil. Messi joins Paris Saint-Germain. Seventh Ballon d'Or
  • 2022: Lionel Messi wins the 2002 World Cup final in Qatar against France, being awarded as the best player of the tournament for the second time. Second The Best FIFA Men's Player.
